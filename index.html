Quick Answers

COMMON QUESTIONS

Do I have to sign a contract?

Nope. We don't do contracts. You call when you need us, we show up. If you don't like our work, you don't have to call again. Simple.

Where do you guys work?

All over Saskatoon — Stonebridge, Willowgrove, Briarwood, Evergreen, you name it. We also cover Warman, Martensville, and Dalmeny.

How do I pay you?

E-Transfer is easiest, but we take cash too. We always send you an invoice by email first so you know exactly what you're paying for.

You actually work in winter?

Yeah, that's kind of our thing. Snow removal is 24/7 from November through whenever spring decides to show up. We've been out at 4am more times than we can count.

How fast can you get here?

For snow, usually same day — often within a few hours. For lawn care and cleanups, we can typically get you on the schedule within 2-3 days.

What if something gets damaged?

We're fully insured, so you're covered. But honestly, we're careful with your property. We treat your yard like it's our own.
