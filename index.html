Quick Answers
FAQ
Do I have to sign a contract?
Nope. You call when you need us, we show up. If you don't like our work, you don't have to call again. Simple.
Where do you work?
All over Saskatoon — Stonebridge, Willowgrove, Briarwood, Evergreen, and more. We also cover Warman, Martensville, and Dalmeny.
How do I pay you?
E-Transfer is easiest, but we take cash too. We always send an invoice by email first so you know exactly what you're paying for.
You actually work in winter?
Yeah, that's kind of our thing. Snow removal is 24/7 from November until spring. We've been out at 4am more times than we can count.
How fast can you get here?
For snow, usually same day — often within a few hours. For lawn care and cleanups, typically within 2-3 days.
What if something gets damaged?
We're fully insured so you're covered. Honestly though — we're careful. We treat your yard like it's our own.
