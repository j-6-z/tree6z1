Got Questions
FAQ
Do I have to sign a contract?
Nope. You call when you need us, we show up. If you don't like our work, you don't have to call again. Simple. We think contracts are a way for companies to lock you in when their service isn't good enough to keep you around naturally.
Where do you work?
All over Saskatoon — Stonebridge, Willowgrove, Briarwood, Evergreen, Hampton Village, Rosewood, and more. We also cover Warman, Martensville, and Dalmeny. Not sure if you're in range? Just call us.
How do I pay you?
E-Transfer is easiest and what most clients use. We also take cash. We always send you an invoice by email before payment so you know exactly what you're paying for — no surprises.
You actually work in winter?
Yeah, that's kind of our thing. Snow removal is 24/7 from first snowfall in November right through until spring. We've been out at 4am more times than we can count. Call us during a blizzard — we answer.
How fast can you get here for snow?
Same day, usually within a few hours depending on how many calls we have. For big dumps we prioritize clients in order of calls received. We don't promise a specific time but we don't leave anyone hanging either.
How often do you mow?
Most clients do weekly or every two weeks during the growing season. We'll tell you honestly what your lawn actually needs — we won't push weekly visits if bi-weekly is fine for your grass type.
What's included in spring cleanup?
Debris removal, dethatching, aeration, fertilization, and edge cleanup. Basically everything your lawn needs after five months under Saskatchewan snow. We assess what your yard actually needs before we start and give you a clear quote.
What if something gets damaged?
We're fully insured so you're covered. Honestly though — we're careful. We flag anything we notice when we arrive so there's no dispute later. We treat your property like it's our own.
Are you licensed with the City of Saskatoon?
Yes. We're registered as a business with the City of Saskatoon and carry full liability insurance. We're not some random guys with a truck — we run a proper operation and we'll show you our license if you want to see it.
Do you do commercial properties?
We primarily serve residential properties but we do take on select small commercial jobs. Give us a call and describe the property — if it's a good fit we'll make it work.
