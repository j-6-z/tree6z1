Know Your Lawn

SEASONAL CARE

SPRING

Dethatch and aerate first. Overseed bare spots. First mow when grass hits 3 inches.

SUMMER

Mow weekly. Never cut more than 1/3 of blade height. Water deep, 1-2x per week.

FALL

Keep mowing until first freeze. Fall fertilization matters. Final mow at 2.5 inches.

WINTER

Clear debris so mold doesn't form. Don't walk on frozen grass. Book spring early.
