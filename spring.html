What We Do

THE SERVICES

DETHATCHING

Power rakes remove dead grass buildup. Lets oxygen reach roots. Your grass grows thicker.

AERATION

Pulls small plugs from soil to reduce compaction. Water and nutrients reach deep. The #1 service for healthy grass.

FERTILIZER

Slow-release nitrogen for spring growth. Green-up in 7-10 days. Lasts 8-10 weeks.
